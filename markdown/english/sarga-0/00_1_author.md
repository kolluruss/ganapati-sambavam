## About the translator

*Kolluru Seetarama Sarma, Smt. Saroja, Chi. Shreyas Sarma*

![](../../../images/family_seetarama.png)


**Seetarama Sarma** is a seasoned technology professional with decades of experience in leadership roles, including Principal Engineer at AWS and Director of Cloud Engineering at U.S. Bank. A graduate of the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Madras, where he earned his Bachelor’s degree in Civil Engineering in 1995, he combines technical expertise with a deep-rooted passion for classical learning.

He holds a Master’s degree in Samskṛtam from the **Hindu University of America** and comes from a distinguished lineage of Sanskrit scholars. Under the guidance of his father, **Sri Kolluru Avatara Sarma**—an esteemed teacher of over four decades and author of more than sixty works—he has cultivated a lifelong dedication to the study and preservation of Sanskrit.
